# 🔥 PassionCast — AI-Powered World Cup Hype Generator

> *Your personal AI commentator that turns World Cup passion into dramatic audio.*

![PassionCast Banner](./screenshot.png)

## What It Does

PassionCast generates passionate, emotionally-charged audio commentary for your World Cup 2026 team. Choose your nation, pick a passion mode (hype speech, glory moment, rivalry fire, heartbreak, fan anthem), select a voice style, and get a unique AI-generated audio commentary that captures the fire of football fandom.

## How It Works

1. **Pick your team** — 48 World Cup 2026 nations
2. **Choose your passion** — Pre-match hype, glory moments, rivalry fire, heartbreak, fan anthems, or custom
3. **Select a voice** — Epic narrator, match commentator, or poetic storyteller
4. **Generate** — Google AI writes the script, ElevenLabs brings it to life

## Tech Stack

- **Google AI (Gemini 2.0 Flash)** — Generates passionate, contextual scripts with real team history, players, and football culture
- **ElevenLabs** — Converts scripts to dramatic, emotionally expressive audio commentary
- **Vanilla JavaScript + Vite** — Fast, lightweight, no framework bloat
- **Client-side only** — No backend needed. API calls go directly from the browser.
